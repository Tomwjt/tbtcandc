Introducing Thoughts Become Things


Hi There

I’m Sue Jones, creator of Thoughts Become Things Coaching and Consulting.

The name “Thoughts Become Things” is very much a part of my journey to setting up this business. The Franchise I was working for (Home Instead Senior Care UK) was embarking on a 360 Leadership programme in 2017. Within this programme there was a video by Mike Dooley whose main message is that the thoughts you have create your own reality. Link is here!
Whilst launching this Programme my thought was that I want to do this for myself, without the confines of an organisation. I wanted to set up my own business enabling as many leaders and business owners to make better decisions for their business. So my thought has become a thing, I did it! It’s a real entity.

So what do I do ……

My passion is working with business owners and leaders to not only get the best out of themselves but also the people within their business.
This is done by reviewing three key areas.

  • Leadership
  • Process
  • People

Within this website I want to provide you with everything you need to know to run a successful business. This will be based on a raft of experience from the franchise world, home care and financial services.

Experience - from most recent

  • Three years working at a multi award winning franchise as a business performance manager supporting over 30 business owners
  • Implementation of a coaching programme within the Franchise network
  • Transitioning businesses from a transactional based service to fee-based service within both home care and financial services
  • Delivery of a change management programme transitioning over 400 financial advisers in the UK to be ready for the Retail Distribution review through one on one consultancy & workshops
  • International experience of working with advisers in Ireland, South Africa, Australia and Hungary
